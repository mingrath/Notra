# AIA Smart Advisor

> AI-powered sales assistant for insurance agents

## Problem
Insurance agents need to manage complex product information and customer needs simultaneously during the sales process. This can lead to inefficiencies and missed opportunities.

## Solution
An AI-powered sales assistant that helps agents provide better advice and close deals more effectively. It streamlines the sales workflow by providing intelligent insights and recommendations.

## Tech Stack
- **TypeScript**: Core language for building the intelligent sales assistant.

## Key Features
- AI-driven sales insights
- Intelligent product recommendations
- Streamlined sales workflow

## Highlights
A tool designed to enhance the productivity and effectiveness of insurance sales professionals. It uses AI to provide a competitive edge in the sales process.
