# AIA Coach

> AI training companion for new insurance agents

## Problem
New insurance agents often face a steep learning curve and need consistent guidance during their initial training. Traditional training methods may lack personalized, real-time support.

## Solution
An AI-powered training companion that provides personalized coaching and support for new agents. It helps them navigate the complexities of the insurance industry through interactive learning.

## Tech Stack
- **TypeScript**: Used for building a reliable and scalable coaching platform.

## Key Features
- Personalized AI coaching
- Real-time training support
- Interactive learning modules

## Highlights
A dedicated tool for empowering new insurance professionals. It leverages AI to provide a consistent and accessible training experience.
