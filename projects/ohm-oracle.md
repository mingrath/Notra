# Ohm Oracle

> Personal knowledge and decision support system

## Problem
Managing personal knowledge and making informed decisions can be difficult with fragmented information. A centralized system is needed to provide intelligent support and insights.

## Solution
A personal knowledge and decision support system that uses AI to organize information and provide actionable insights. It helps in making better decisions by leveraging a structured knowledge base.

## Tech Stack
- **AI**: Core technology for providing intelligent support and decision-making assistance.

## Key Features
- Personal knowledge management
- AI-driven decision support
- Centralized information hub

## Highlights
A personal utility for enhancing cognitive performance and decision-making. It serves as an intelligent companion for managing personal information.
