# Vetsikkha App

> AI veterinary consultant web app (webchat + Telegram)

## Problem
Veterinary professionals and pet owners need quick access to reliable information and consultation. Traditional methods can be slow and may not be available 24/7.

## Solution
An AI-powered veterinary consultant web app that provides instant support via webchat and Telegram. It offers a convenient and accessible way to get veterinary advice and information.

## Tech Stack
- **Python**: Core language for building the AI consultant and chatbot integrations.

## Key Features
- AI-driven veterinary consultation
- Webchat and Telegram integration
- 24/7 accessibility

## Highlights
A versatile tool for the veterinary community. It leverages AI to provide timely and accessible support for veterinary-related inquiries.
